Data Processing Agreement

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. Scope and Purpose

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") governs the processing of personal data by OrgsLedger on behalf of organizations using our platform.

2. Definitions

  • Data Controller: Your organization
  • Data Processor: OrgsLedger
  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable person

3. Processing Instructions

OrgsLedger will only process personal data according to your documented instructions and for the purposes of providing the Service.

4. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encryption of data at rest and in transit
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Sub-processors

We may engage sub-processors to assist in providing the Service. Current sub-processors include cloud hosting and database providers.

6. Data Subject Rights

We will assist you in responding to requests from data subjects exercising their rights under applicable data protection laws.

7. Data Breach Notification

We will notify you of any personal data breach without undue delay after becoming aware of such breach.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Upon termination of services, we will delete or return all personal data as per your instructions.

9. Contact

For DPA inquiries, contact us at support@orgsledger.com.
