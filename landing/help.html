Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite members?

Go to Members → Invite, generate an invite link, and share it with your members. They can join by clicking the link and creating an account.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes.

Can I export my data?

Yes, go to Settings → Data → Export to download your organization's data in CSV or PDF format.

How do I change my subscription plan?

Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan to upgrade or downgrade your subscription.
