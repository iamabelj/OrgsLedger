Our Story

Born From a Real Problem

OrgsLedger started from a simple frustration: managing a cross-border community organization meant juggling WhatsApp groups, spreadsheets, bank transfers, and paper receipts — with zero accountability and maximum confusion.

Existing tools were either too corporate (and expensive) or too simplistic. None understood the unique challenges of organizations that operate across borders — multiple currencies, language barriers, members in different time zones, and the absolute need for financial transparency.

So we built OrgsLedger: a single platform where organizations can manage their entire operation — from collecting dues in multiple currencies, to running meetings with AI-generated summaries, to keeping every document secure and accessible.
