Features

Everything Your Organization Needs, All in One Place

From financial tracking to AI-powered meeting summaries — OrgsLedger replaces a dozen tools with one streamlined platform.

💰

Financial Management

Track dues, levies, donations, and expenses. Automated recurring billing, payment integration with Paystack & Flutterwave, and real-time financial dashboards.

💬

Real-Time Chat & Channels

Dedicated chat channels, direct messages, read receipts, and file sharing. Built with Socket.io for instant, real-time communication.

📅

Meeting Scheduler

Schedule meetings, send automatic reminders, track attendance, and generate AI-powered meeting minutes from audio recordings.

🤖

AI-Powered Insights

GPT-4o integration for meeting summaries, financial analysis, speech-to-text transcription, and intelligent document generation.

📊

Analytics & Reports

Revenue breakdowns, member activity trends, payment analytics, and exportable reports. Data-driven decisions at your fingertips.

👥

Member Management

Role-based access control, committee management, member profiles, activity tracking, and hierarchical permissions.

📢

Announcements & Events

Organization-wide announcements, event scheduling with RSVP tracking, push notifications, and email alerts.

🗳️

Polls & Voting

Create polls, conduct elections, and gather member opinions. Real-time results with anonymous voting options.

📁

Document Vault

Secure document storage, file sharing, version control, and organizing important files by categories and access levels.
