Getting Started

Launch Your Organization in Minutes

From sign-up to fully operational workspace — no installation, no setup complexity, no servers to manage.

Create Your Organization

Sign up at app.orgsledger.com and create your organization. Name it, describe it, configure your structure.

2

Select Your Annual Plan

Choose Standard, Professional, or Enterprise based on your size and needs. Pay annually via Stripe (USD) or Paystack (NGN).

A secure, isolated workspace is instantly created with all features enabled based on your plan tier. No waiting.

A unique invite link is generated. Share it with members via email, WhatsApp, or any channel. They join in seconds.

Members sign up, accept the invite, and immediately access chat, meetings, polls, events, documents, and financials.

Optionally top up your AI and Translation wallets. Enable AI meeting summaries and real-time translation on demand — pay only for what you use.
